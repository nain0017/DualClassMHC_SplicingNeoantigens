# Figures

Main-manuscript figures (fig1 to fig5) and supplementary figures (s1 to s5) as embedded in the manuscript and supplementary. Filenames match the figure numbers as they appear in the manuscript.

## Main figures

| File | Manuscript label | Content |
|------|------------------|---------|
| `fig1.png` | Figure 1 | Pipeline schematic: three-cohort design through STAR alignment, LeafCutter-DS differential intron usage, Stouffer meta-analysis, SpliceMutr-adapted peptide prediction, and downstream response, survival, TMB, and neoantigen load orthogonality analyses. |
| `fig2.png` | Figure 2 | Discovery landscape: funnel from 137 meta-significant cluster-tuples to 20 dual-class binder cluster-tuples mapping to 21 named gene symbols; per-gene class I and class II binder counts; functional categorization of hit genes. |
| `fig3.png` | Figure 3 | Two orthogonality findings: SA versus TMB and SA versus published mutation-derived neoantigen load, per cohort, for SA class I, SA class II, and SA combined. All per-cohort Spearman rho within the pre-specified orthogonal zone (absolute rho less than 0.3). |
| `fig4.png` | Figure 4 | Survival analysis: Kaplan-Meier overall survival for SA class II high versus low in each cohort; Gide-specific progression-free survival; Cox univariate forest plot across all three SA metrics. Gide OS HR = 2.61 (95% CI 1.56 to 4.38, p = 0.0003); Gide PFS HR = 1.80 (1.17 to 2.77, p = 0.0075). |
| `fig5.png` | Figure 5 | R versus NR splicing antigenicity distributions: per-sample SA score across three cohorts and three SA metrics with per-cohort Mann-Whitney U p values and cross-cohort Stouffer combined p values. |
