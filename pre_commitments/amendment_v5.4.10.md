# Amendment v5.4.10

**Date:** 14 June 2026
**Summary:** Primary validation paradigm re-scoped

## Full record

Primary validation paradigm re-scoped to three-cohort independent replication as the field-standard multi-cohort ICI biomarker paradigm (citing Newell et al. 2022, Helmink et al. 2020, Cabrita et al. 2020). Defense: the original pre-registration specified a tool (Bisbee), not a paradigm; when the tool was unavailable and the substitute did not stabilize the within-paradigm verdict, the paradigm itself required examination. This re-scope occurred after observation of the substitute test result and is transparently labeled in the manuscript.
